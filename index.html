TKT Contracting

Proudly Serving Whistler and the Surrounding Area

Following an extensive career in the excavation and civil project industry, Timothy (Tim) Thompson founded TKT Contracting Ltd. in March 1998. The establishing of the company was, and continues to be the provision of excellent customer service by professional and committed personnel. To this end, we provide estimates for particular projects in a detailed and understandable manner with realistic completion dates. When Favored with contracts, we work diligently to ensure completion in a timely and efficient manner while always carrying out our work in a competitive and collaborative way. Due to the civil projects being weather sensitive, the company also entered into the snow clearing business for the winter months. The same committed and professional personnel carry out these duties with the same customer service attitude. It has been due to the customer being of primary concern that TKT Contracting Ltd. has been able to grow its business. We have varied inventory of equipment for the scope of work that we perform. This inventory includes Excavators, Backhoes, Dump Trucks, Loaders, Sand and Snow Plowing Equipment. We are also prepared to make any additions that might be required for specific circumstances. We would be pleased to provide references and contacts who could provide you with an honest opinion of our capabilities and a description of projects carried out on their behalf.
